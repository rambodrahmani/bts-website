Europe's leading cross-border recruiting conference.

Connecting Europe’s best talents with Europe’s best companies.

For three days in April 2016, 300 of the best talents, students, graduates and young professionals from all over Europe will come to Berlin and meet Europe's most successful companies and startups.

The Berlin Talent Summit stands out from all the other startup conferences and career fairs. Our guiding principle – in a nutshell – can be summarized as curated recruiting: from the pre-selection of the participants and companies to the creation of multifaceted opportunities for deliberate and thorough interaction and engagement. We have one goal for the BTS – we want you to meet, get to know and hire Europe’s best talents all while minimizing your recruiting and employer branding efforts.

The Berlin Talent Summit in a nutshell

More than just a startup conference.

Europe’s best talents

Screened and selected based on their achievements and motivation, coming from 40+ target universities inside and outside of Germany and having a multitude of backgrounds.

Quality instead of quantity

We are not the next impersonal and overcrowded career fair. Through our rigorous selection process we will make sure you can focus your resources on meeting Europe’s very best.

Our mission

Connecting the best talents with the best companies. And that doesn’t mean for us to just provide you with a 2x2 meter booth right next to 200 other companies. It means to offer valuable opportunities for direct engagement, such as collaborative workshop sessions, relaxed networking events and inspiring ge-togethers.

Our idea

We want you to show the participants what your company is all about: your values, your work environment and your daily routines. Because that is what will make the participants wanting to stay right at your office.

Exclusive and curated recruiting of high potential students, graduates and young professionals. Europe’s best candidates, right at your doorstep.

    Participants

    More than just your regular recruiting event participants

    Meet Europe's best talents right at your doorstep in Berlin.
    To guarantee the exclusivity of the Berlin Talent Summit and the quality of our participants, we have initiated a rigorous selection process. Each candidate has to apply with their CV and a letter of motivation, which will then be ranked along a multitude of criteria against all other applicants. Only then the top applicants will receive their letter of admission.

    For the 2015 Berlin Talent Summit, we received around 500 applications, of which we selected the 200 best candidates. Around 50% of the candidates had a business or finance background, one third of the candidates came from a tech background while the remainder had a variety of backgrounds (from law to marketing and visual arts).

    1. Keynotes and Presentations

    Address the entire audience or a selected subgroup with a topic of your choice in an innovative presentation format – bring your best speaker and get prepared, because the questions will just keep on coming.

    • Recruiting: 0/3
    • Employer Branding: 3/3

    2. Office Visit and In-company Workshop

    If you are a Berlin-based company, you can host a workshop right at your office-for 2 hours (or even more), you can tackle and work on real life challenges with the group, no matter if its a marketing plan, expansion strategy or growth hack. The participants will see in-depth how your company works and what it feels lile to be part of your team, and you can find out in detail who has the qualities to become a new team member.

    • Recruiting: 1/3
    • Employer Branding: 2/3

    3. Workshop

    If your company is not based in Berlin – no worries! We don’t want you to miss out on the opportunity of hosting a workshop, and – in collaboration with our location partners in Berlin – organise you space in some of the hippest working locations in Berlin. We have done this before with companies from Hamburg and Munich, and it worked out just as well. Small advantage: that way you have the chance to impress even more with the content, because the participants won’t be distracted by the kicker, pool or open bar you have in your office;)

    • Recruiting: 1/3
    • Employer Branding: 2/3

    4. Interviews

    Don’t miss out on the chance to directly interview and recruit some of Europe’s best talents- at the end of the day, that’s what the BTS is all about. We will provide you with the CVs of the selected participants prior to the BTS16, so you can have a first check and even schedule interviews before the candidates will land in Berlin. During the BTS, we will then provide you with dedicated time slots and exclusive interview rooms, so you can conduct your conversations in private. Believe us- the only regret you will have is that you don’t have time to interview all 300 participants. In the last years, some participants directly signed their contracts at the spot or during the afterparty. Convenient, isn’t it?

    • Recruiting: 3/3
    • Employer Branding: 0/3

    5. Speed-dating & Recruiting Corner

    If you don’t have the resources to look through the CVs in advance or you prefer to get surprised by the quality of the participants during the BTS, you can also participate in our speed-dating sessions, where you will have the chance to meet a large number of participants in a very short time. If you have found a candidate with whom you would like to continue your conversation, you can retreat to your private recruiting corner to do so. The best part: these recruiting corners have nothing to do with the usual impersonal and standardized boothes at career fairs, yet are relaxing couch areas that provide a totally new recuriting spirit in which new contacts are made easily and conversations freed of the usual career fair stiffness.

    • Recruiting: 3/3
    • Employer Branding: 0/3

    6. Networking activities

    Based on the mission of the BTS, we want to create as much interaction between the participants and the companies as possible. Therefore, we will organize plenty of networking opportunities besides the more formal program points- a food market, a BBQ, an afterparty, a soccer match or a cruise on the river Spree, just to name a few. At the end of the day, the best connections are being made with a beer in your hand.

    • Recruiting: 2/3
    • Employer Branding: 1/3

    7. Skill sessions

    We want the participants to not just go home with many insights about our great partner companies and multiple job offers in their hands, we also want to make them better during the BTS: better candidates during job interviews, better presenters and public speakers, better networkers. Therefore we will organize hands-on skill sessions that target these super important issues of individual career development: CV checks, mock interviews, presentation exercises and many more innovative formats. During these sessions you can observe first hand some of the most important qualities of a potential employee: the ability to listen and learn, as well as to react to criticism and feedback. What the workshops offer from a content point of view, the skill sessions do from a soft skill perspective.

    • Recruiting: 1/3
    • Employer Branding: 2/3

    8. New concepts

    This time, we will also enrich the BTS by offering new formats and concepts: a hackathon, a „Schnitzeljagd“ and many other new and innovative formats. These program modules will be realized in cooperation with external partners and organized only on-demand by our partners. On that note- if you have any special ideas or program points which you always wanted to experience but never saw realized, please just get in touch. We will organize it specifically for you and make it an unforgettable part of the BTS16.
